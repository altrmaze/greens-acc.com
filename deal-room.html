Legal Agreement & Compliance Declaration

NON-CIRCUMVENTION, NON-DISCLOSURE & WORKING AGREEMENT (NCNDA)

By engaging with the Greens ACC Secure Clearance Platform, all parties agree to maintain strict non-circumvention and non-disclosure of proprietary deal structures, seller identities, buyer contacts, and transaction terms discovered or facilitated through this platform.

Article 1 — Non-Circumvention: No party shall bypass, circumvent, or directly contact any deal counterparty, introduced entity, or proprietary contact without prior written consent from Greens ACC. Violation carries a penalty equal to the full deal commission value or a minimum of USD 50,000.

Article 2 — Non-Disclosure: All transaction data, pricing structures, seller mandates, and buyer requirements are classified as strictly confidential. Unauthorised disclosure is subject to immediate legal action under applicable international commercial law.

Article 3 — Fraudulent Documentation: Submission of falsified, forged, or misrepresented trade documents (including but not limited to SGS certificates, certificates of origin, or letters of intent) constitutes fraud under international trade law. Greens ACC reserves the right to immediately suspend access, report to relevant authorities, and pursue full legal damages.

Article 4 — Platform Exclusion Policy: Greens ACC strictly does not broker, facilitate, list, or process transactions involving fuel, diesel, petroleum, crude oil, or any derivative petroleum commodity. Any attempt to submit fuel-related deals will result in permanent account suspension and regulatory reporting.

Article 5 — Governing Law: This agreement is governed by international commercial arbitration standards (ICC Rules). Disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration.
